Triangle Solver

A Triangle Solver is a tool designed to help you solve various types of problems involving triangles. It allows you to calculate unknown angles and sides of a triangle when given certain known values. Additionally, it can help you determine the type of triangle based on its angles. Whether you're dealing with angles, sides, or both, this tool provides a straightforward way to perform these calculations and obtain accurate results.

Calculate Missing Angle

(Given two angles in a triangle, to find the other angle)

Calculate Missing Side

(Given two sides with angle between them, to find the other side of the triangle)

Calculate Angles from 3 Sides

(Given three sides to find the angles between them)

Calculate Missing Side

(Given three angles with two known sides, to find the other side)

Identify Triangle Type

Enter the three angles of the triangle to determine if it can form a valid triangle and identify its type.
