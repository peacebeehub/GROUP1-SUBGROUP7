Trigonometric Function Grapher

A Trigonometric Function Grapher is a tool that allows users to visualize and explore the graphs of various trigonometric functions like sine, cosine, tangent, and their inverses. It typically includes controls for adjusting the parameters of the functions, such as amplitude, frequency, and phase shift, enabling users to see how these changes affect the shape and behavior of the graphs.

Explore the behavior of trigonometric functions by adjusting parameters and viewing the resulting graphs.
